Easy Fare

Our Mission

We have the goal of making transport around the dublin zone much more transparent to students, visitors, and anyone who is looking to use dublins public services. We aim for giving you a much more simple journey by telling you the exact costs of any travels you may have along with other information useful information about specific stations, leap cards, and rail passes!

About Us

We are a group of inspiring students who have the goal of never letting anyone else miss a train. We know how complicated the fares and times can be but thats why we are here. Thank you so much for choosing us to guild your way.
